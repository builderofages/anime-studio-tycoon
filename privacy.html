Privacy Policy

Anime Studio Tycoon  ·  Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TrainYourAgent ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the game Anime Studio Tycoon (the "Game"), available on the web and as a mobile app. We have designed the Game to collect as little personal data as possible. By playing the Game you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we do not collect

The Game does not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location to play. We do not run our own advertising or sell any personal data. We do not knowingly build advertising profiles about you.

2. Information stored on your device

Your game progress (such as in-game currency, fans, owned items, settings, and purchase unlocks) is saved locally on your own device using browser local storage or the device's app storage. This data stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and you can erase it at any time by clearing your browser/app data or using any in-game reset option.

3. Purchases

Optional in-game purchases (such as the Producer Pass or gem packs) are processed by third-party payment providers, not by us:

  • Web: purchases are handled by Gumroad, Inc. Gumroad collects and processes your payment and contact details under its own privacy policy: gumroad.com/privacy. We receive only limited, non-sensitive confirmation that a purchase occurred (for example, an order reference). We never receive or store your full card number.
  • Mobile apps: purchases are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play under their respective privacy policies. We do not receive your payment card details.

4. Analytics

The Game ships without third-party analytics or tracking SDKs enabled. If we later add privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics, we will update this Policy and, where required, request consent.

5. Children

The Game is suitable for a general audience of all ages. Because we do not collect personal information from any user, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Game contains optional purchases; we recommend parents and guardians enable device-level purchase controls (Screen Time / Family Link) for younger players. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA and similar laws)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to processing or data sale. Because the Game does not collect or store personal data on our servers, in most cases we hold nothing to disclose or delete. Payment-related data is held by Gumroad, Apple, or Google, and you can exercise your rights directly with them. You may also contact us at any time and we will help where we can. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

7. International users

The Game is available globally. Since gameplay data stays on your device and we do not operate user-data servers for the Game, your gameplay information is not transferred internationally by us. Payment processors operate their own global infrastructure under their policies.

8. Security

We rely on your device's and our hosting provider's standard protections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but because we minimise the data involved, the risk to you is low.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued play after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
